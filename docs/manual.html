Workflow Map / 図で見る流れ

A safe automation project is a chain of small decisions.

安全な業務自動化は、いきなり本番化するものではありません。相談、業務選定、サンプル入力、AI下書き、人間承認、提案またはPoCの順に進めます。

Safety / 安全境界

AI can help a lot, but humans keep control of real-world actions.

Human prepares / 人間が決める

Business task, sample input, output goal, client rules, approval owner, and whether to continue.

AI drafts / AIが下書きする

Questions, workflow map, reply drafts, missing-input lists, client explanations, and PoC materials.

Human approves / 人間が止める・承認する

Real sends, bookings, refunds, price changes, production data, secrets, paid cloud, and sensitive decisions.
